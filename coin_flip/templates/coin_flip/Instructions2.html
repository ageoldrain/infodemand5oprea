Informed Consent

As a participant in this experiment, you have the right to know the potential risks and benefits of participating.

The risks of participating in this experiment to you include mild eye fatigue from looking at a screen.

The benefits of participating in this experiment to you are the monetary rewards you will receive.

You can choose to end this experiment at any time, without penalty. If you complete the experiment, you will be paid the base reimbursement of $5, plus any earnings you made during the experiment.
